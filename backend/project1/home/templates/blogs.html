Established On 1977

Welcome to AR Furnishings, where timeless craftsmanship meets the spirit of the '70s. Established in 1977, we take pride in being a cornerstone of quality and style in the furniture industry for over four decades.

#1977

Exapansion

In 2010, AR Furnishings embarked on an exciting journey of expansion, solidifying its position as a leading name in the furniture industry. With a commitment to preserving the legacy of craftsmanship while embracing modern sensibilities, our expansion marked a significant milestone in our storied history.

#2010

Online Presence

Recognizing the changing landscape of retail, AR embraced the digital age by launching a user-friendly online platform. Our website became a virtual showroom, allowing customers to explore our curated collections from the comfort of their homes. The expansion into the online space opened up new avenues for reaching a global audience and connecting with a wider customer base.

#2015

UNLEASHING AUGUMENTED REALITY

In 2020, AR didn't just adopt augmented reality; we embraced it as a tool to enhance customer experience, bridge the gap between physical and digital realms, and pioneer a new era of innovation in the furniture industry. Our commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and community remained unwavering, even as we stepped into the exciting frontier of augmented reality.AR's augmented reality experience transported customers into immersive virtual showrooms. From the comfort of their homes, shoppers could explore our entire collection, virtually placing furniture within their living spaces. This not only enhanced the convenience of the shopping process but also allowed for a more informed and personalized decision-making experience.

#2020
